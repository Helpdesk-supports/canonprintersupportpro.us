Methods To Restarting Canon Print Spooler in Windows 8 And 10

Method 1:

Restart The Print Spooler On Windows 10

Follow the below given steps to fix Print Spooler not running issue on your Windows 10. Have an instant look at once:

  • Press the keys “Windows logo” and “R” simultaneously to open the Run box
  • Now, type “services.msc” and press “Enter” to open the services window
  • After that click “Print Spooler” and then “Restart”
  • Check to see whether your Canon printer works

Method 2:

Run Print Spooler On Windows 7

Try out the below given steps to do it:

  • On your keyboard, press “Windows logo key” and “R” button at the same time to open the Run box
  • After that type “services.msc” and press “Enter” to open the services window
  • Now, right click on the “Print Spooler” button and then press “Properties”
  • Make sure the startup type is set to Automatic and then click “Apply” and “OK”
