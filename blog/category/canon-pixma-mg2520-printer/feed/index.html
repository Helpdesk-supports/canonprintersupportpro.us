Desired Steps To Setup Canon Pixma MG2520 Printer Manually

Here is the step-by-step guide listed you simply has to follow them till the end to effortlessly setup your Canon Pixma Mg2520 All-in-one printer. Give a look at below points:

Setup Canon Pixma MG2520 Printer Manually For Windows

If you are a windows user and have purchased the PIXMA MG2520 printer, then don’t look here and there. Simply, take a glance at given lines and perform the same accordingly:

As early as the printer will be plugged-in to the operating system, the window will be active sooner and thus will start installing the machine for further use

  • Do not turn your Canon Pixma Mg2520 printer ON until the system being stated
  • When you unbox the printer, you will get to see the USB cable that has come along with the package
  • What you should actually do is to plug the right-end of the USB cable to the USB port on your printer and then plug the remaining end of the USB cable to the free USB port to the PC
  • After successfully plugged-in the USB cable, turn your printer ON by pressing the power button on your machine
  • After that, you are advisable to do the desktop and then tap the “Start” button
  • By doing this, Control Panel window will be opened. So, on this page, you have to hit the “Device and Printers” option and then click on “Add a printer” option
  • After that move ahead to the local printer option
  • And then search and hit the port where your printer is connected to the PC
  • In the upcoming step the Canon option will pop-up on Windows screen. You have to select it and then choose your printer model. Once you select the right printer from the list, click “Next” button
  • Click “Next” button again
  • And then tap “Finish” to keep your selected printer option as a default
  • The printer setup process gets done; your printer is ready to print now. Enjoy!

Setup Canon Pixma MG2520 Printer Manually For Mac

For Mac users, skip the above written steps and focus on the underneath points as the following guides will be impactful for you. Let’s start the Canon Pixma MG2520 Printer Setup process:

  • First and foremost, users are required to check the top-right corner of the website and locate the “Support & Drivers” section from there
  • Next, you have to go to the list of the product manner and there look at for “consumer and home office” option. Hit on it once you found
  • After that, go the list of the product family and then select the “Printers” option
  • Click on the “Drivers and software” option next to the Canon Pixma MG2520
  • After doing that, a drop-down menu will be seen. By clicking on it you have to choose the version of Mac OS that you are using
  • And then start downloading the printer driver on Mac
  • Once download completes, give a double-click on the downloaded file to run it
  • Select the “Continue” button twice and then press “Install” to add the printer on your Mac device
  • Then follow the on-screen prompts appropriately to install the driver
  • At the end, use USB cable to connect your printer with Mac device
  • Now, your printer is all set to deliver the printouts. Enjoy!
