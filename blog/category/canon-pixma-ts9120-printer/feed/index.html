]]> Following the below-mentioned procedure you can easily connect your Canon Pixma TS9120 printer to your Windows OS computer:

Setting Wireless connectivity on your Canon Pixma TS9120 printer

  • Switch On the power to your Canon TS9120 setup
  • Ensure that the printer is properly switched on by confirming with the turn On light on the printer
  • If properly switched on, Click on the Canon Pixma icon that will be visible on the left side of the display screen
  • If any error is encountered, Click on OK and then select the icon
  • Following the above steps, select the network icon and then chose the network settings option
  • Select the LAN settings to option from the basic information section
  • Click on the Wireless LAN followed by selecting its set up to start the process
  • Select the ‘Easy Wireless Connect’ option and then select the ‘Yes’ option
  • When the steps from printer’s end are completed, a message will arrive on the printer screen having the instructions to complete the process

 

Getting the connection setup on your Windows computer

  • Visit the Canon official website and navigate to its official page
  • Select the download button to initiate the setup and download process
  • After the downloading is completed, navigate to the folder where the setup file is downloaded on your computer
  • Once found, open the setup software file, then click on the ‘Start Setup’ button
  • After the setup window appears on the screen, select your country/region and then click on ‘Next’ to proceed
  • In the next step carefully read the License agreement and provide your consent to it subsequently by clicking on the ‘yes’ option available
  • Select the ‘Deactivate your block’ option and then click on the ‘Next’ option
  • To choose a WiFi network as your connection method click on the ‘Yes’ button
  • After the above steps, select your WiFi network and connect to it by providing the network key for it
  • Once done click on the ‘Next’ button
  • Complete the printer set-up by then selecting your Canon TS9120 setup as default
  • Click on ‘Next’ to skip the next steps and at the end select the ‘exit’ button to conclude the setup process

Connecting your Canon Pixma TS9120 printer to your MacOS Computer using WiFi

Following the below-mentioned procedure you can easily connect your Canon TS9120 setup to your Mac OS computer:

Setting Wireless connectivity on your Canon Pixma TS9120 printer

  • Switch On the power to your Canon Pixma TS9120 printer
  • Ensure that the printer is properly switched on by confirming with the turn On light on the printer
  • If properly switched on, Click on the Canon Pixma icon that will be visible on the left side of the display screen
  • Following the above steps, select the network icon and then chose the network settings option
  • Select the LAN settings to option from the basic information section
  • Click on the Wireless LAN followed by selecting its set up to start the process
  • Select the ‘Easy Wireless Connect’ option and then select the ‘Yes’ option
  • When the steps from printer’s end are completed, a message will arrive on the printer screen having the instructions to complete the process

Getting the connection setup on your macOS computer

  • Visit the Canon official website and navigate to its official page
  • Select the download button to initiate the setup and download process
  • Once the downloading is completed open the setup file manually if it does not open automatically on your Mac device
  • Click on the ‘Next’ button to proceed in the setup process
  • Enter the username and password of your MAC device once the pop-up appears and then choose the ‘Install Helper’ option
  • Click on the ‘Start Setup’ option to proceed
  • Select your location’s region/country and then select the ‘next’ option
  • In the next step carefully read the License agreement and provide your consent to it subsequently by clicking on the ‘yes’ option available
  • Select your printer setup process and then click on ‘Yes’ on the printer connection method option
  • Select your WiFi network from the enlisted networks identified by the device and then enter the appropriate network key
  • Install the printer drivers for your MAC device by clicking on the ‘Next’ button
  • Click on the Allow button on your MAC to provide the access key “My Network” in your keychain
  • Click on add printer and then select the Canon TS series printer
  • From the dropdown select ‘Airprint’ option and by clicking the ‘Next’ button add the printer to your MAC device
  • Click on ‘next’ button to skip the following steps and then click on the ‘Exit’ button to complete the setup process

If even after following the aforementioned steps you are unable to connect your Canon Pixma TS9120 printer to WiFi then you can connect with the highly skilled Canon Support team for their assistance.
