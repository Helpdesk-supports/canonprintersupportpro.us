Download The Canon Printer Drivers:

Refer to following steps to download Canon Printer drivers manually directly from the online source:

  • After that, you also need to choose the operating system.

  • Once you complete the downloading, you will have the save the setup file on your computer system.

Follow the on-screen instructions and you will successfully install the driver for your Canon printer. Besides, you should also get to know about some essential information while downloading the right driver manually.

Keep The Significant Information Into Consideration:

After downloading the printer drivers successfully, you should update these drivers to replace the older version with the new ones. For that, you should have to use device manager and follow some instructions listed below:

Update Canon Printer Drivers:  

  • Press Windows ‘key’ and ‘R’ button simultaneously to open the ‘Run’ window.
  • In the dialogue box, you should type “devmgmt.msc”
  • Next, you need to look for your printer which is available under ‘Print Queues’.
  • Once you find out the canon printer, double-click on it.
  • On the menu section, go to the ‘Update Driver’ option.
  • Select ‘Search automatically’ option to update driver software.
  • After that, you should give a quick restart to your computer system.

How Do I Install Canon Printer Drivers?

If you want to install Canon printer drivers, you should reach out to PC. After that, by press Window button, open the ‘Start menu’. Now, you should opt for the ‘Devices’ option. In the next window, you should choose the ‘Printers’ option. Hereafter, an option says ‘Add Printer’ option appears, click on it. Once done, you should click on ‘Add a Local Printer’ and select the Next option. Select the ‘Printer Port’ and you will be able to successfully install Canon printer drivers.
