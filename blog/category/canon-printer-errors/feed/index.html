Universal Troubleshooting Guides For Canon Printer Error U052

Mainly, Canon Printer Error U052 occurs in two different scenarios. One is where the print head is not installed at all and then throwing ‘U052, This type of print head is incorrect’ error message. And the second scenario is where the print head is installed but it is not functioning well or probably not working. The below section will show you step by step guide to annihilate both the scenarios separately. Let’s begin the process:

Fix The Error U052 with Print Head Installed

  • Open the top lid of the printer to access the ink cartridges and the print head. Once you open the top lid, the cartridges slot will automatically lift up in the middle front of the printer
  • Unplug the cable linked to printer from the switch board. Make sure the cartridge knob placed just beside the cartridge cradle which is lifted up. In this position of the lever, the cartridge slot will not auto move
  • Take out all the ink cartridges from the cartridges slot
  • Removal of cartridges will uncover the black print head. Now, slightly remove the print head from the printer by gently pulling the inserted cartridges
  • And then, clean the print head with a soft cotton cloth
  • Now, Reinsert the print head and the ink cartridges properly into the printer
  • Close the top lid of the printer and switch-on your printer
  • Try to print in order to check whether the Error U052 resolves or continue persisting

Fix The Error U052 Without Any Print Head Installed

Here are the steps you should go through to solve it:

  • First, turn your printer off
  • Next, open the top lid of the printer. If there are no cartridges or print head installed in the printer, then the cartridges slots will naturally located in the middle front of the printer
  • Install the print head into the sliding rail just beside the cartridges slot
  • Install the cartridges into the cartridge slots. Close the lever and ensure that there is no door open and the cartridges and the print heads are properly installed
  • Close the top lid of the printer and then turn the printer on
  • Try to print a test page to check the Canon Printer Error U052 if it sorted out or not

Fix Canon Printer Error 6c10 With Easy Steps

Step 1:

  • Move Printerhead to the center
  • Disconnect the printer and then check on the right side- is the purge unit in the right condition.
  • No piece of paper should be there.

Step 2:

  • Reset your printer
  • Now power off and then wait for a few minutes.
  • Now switch on the printer
  • Keep holding the Resume button for 15 to 20 seconds.

Step 3:

  • Switch off your Canon printer.
  • Now open its door.
  • Power on your device.
  • When you see that the cartridges are in the center then close the door.

Step 4:

  • Open the lid and allow your ink cartridge carrier to center it.
  • By looking at the right side you will notice a row of white rollers behind the carrier.
  • At the end of the rollers, you will see a small, clear piece of plastic that sticks up.
  • Now you need to take a small piece of towel and slowly wipe all of the ink.
  • Remember to you cotton cloth or paper towel.
  • Once the rollers are cleaned move on to the ink pad section.
  • About a 1 or 2 inches in the direction of the printer from that piece of plastic, you will see a small square. And about half inches per side you will find a small form pad.
  • This is the place where you will see that the excess of ink gets collected. Now you need to soak it.
  • Use another paper towel and press it into its foam. Keep doing that until the paper towel is no stop collecting the ink.
  • Now close the lid and power off the printer then turn it back on.

Step 5:

Next, you need to clean your Canon printer’s Printerhead. For this, you can take the help of your Canon printer manual and read the guide from the manual and follow the step by step instructions.

Step 6:

  • Remove the casing and look for any micro switch that is being pressed by one of the purge unit cams.
  • Now you need to clean it.

Reset The Ink Absorber Counter:

  • Switch off the printer.
  • Press and hold the Resume button for a few minutes.
  • Now press and hold the Power button at the same time. It will turn On the green LED light.
  • Hold the power button and release the resume button
  • Press the Resume button two times, and then release both the buttons.
  • Now press the resume button four-time and then immediately press the Power button.

This sequence of pressing and releasing power and resume button will reset the ink absorber counter on the printer. Now after this switch off the printer unplug it and then plug it again and power on the printer. Now check if the printer is out of this 6c10 error or not.

Conclusion:

After the whole process restarts your printer and takes a closer look while printing. If it prints perfectly then the error is solved, but if it miss-print or deny from printing, it means the error is still present.
