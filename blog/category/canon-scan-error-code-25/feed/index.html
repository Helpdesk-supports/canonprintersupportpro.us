Solutions To Fix Canon Scan Error Code 255, 0, 0

There is nothing more exasperating than having a scanner error. When you try to scan an important document, this error can seriously make you driven up the wall. But you need not feel blue anymore. With simple and easy solutions, you can make this error annihilate. Take help from the blog or connect with qualified engineers’ team get to the roots of the error.

Follow the below discussed solutions to take care of the error with efficiency:

Solution 1

Making Use Of MF Toolbox: Follow the instructions noted below to perform this task

  • First thing you have to do is to ensure that whether the MF Toolbox is closed on the computer or not
  • Next, you need to press the “Scan” button on the printer and thus go ahead towards the next step
  • Go to “MF Toolbox” on the computer and choose the PDF option
  • Click on the green button or the Scan button on the PDF window
  • Finally, your Canon Printer will start scanning the document

Solution 2

Change System Settings: If the above solution is not effectual for you, then make changes in settings on your computer system. To it with the below noted steps:

  • First and foremost you will have to log into your system as an administrator using Remote UI
  • Go to the option“System Settings” and thus turn off “USB Device Settings: Use USB Device” option
  • Finally click “OK” button to make the changes saved
  • Now turn-on your printer and thenoff again to repair this error
