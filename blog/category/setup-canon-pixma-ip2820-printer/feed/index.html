Desired Steps To Setup Canon Pixma Ip2820 Printer Manually

When you go through the below-noted instructions, you can effortlessly setup your Canon Pixma Ip2820 Printer Manually. So, have a glimpse at once and try to follow the steps:

  • The very beginning process is you have to keep your new canon Pixma ip2820 printer on a flat surface or table so you can handle it easily
  • Now, you have to install the ink cartridges into the required slots
  • Then, you have to load the stack of white plain A-4 sized paper into the paper tray
  • After that, use USB cable that came with the packaging materials to connect the printer to your computer

Connecting Canon Pixma iP2820 Printer with Computer Using the USB Cable

Here are the steps to connect your printer with the operating system via USB cable. Follow them one-by-one:

  • First and forecast, you are suggested to turn ON your printer by pressing the Power button
  • Next, connect the flat end of the USB cable to the computer and the square end of the USB cable to the printer
  • Now a message “new hardware found” will pop-up on the window screen
  • Go to that message and follow the prompted instructions to appropriately connect the printer with your PC
  • Once both the devices are connected with each other, your printer is then ready to print

Canon Pixma iP2820 Printer Wireless Setup Process

It is essential to configure your printer for wireless usages. Follow the given steps for canon Pixma ip2820 printer for wireless setup:

  • First, turn on your printer
  • Then press the “Setup” button on your printer display screen
  • Choose the wireless LAN setup option and then tap “OK”
  • Now you can connect your Canon Pixma printer to Wi-Fi
  • The printer will start searching for the available networks
  • The printer will then start wirelessly setup. This process may take a few minute so wait for sometime
