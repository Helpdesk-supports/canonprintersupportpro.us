]]> Before starting, make sure that you have installed the printer using the same Wi-Fi network as that of your laptop/ desktop. You also need to check that the printer’s access plug must have a physical WPS push control. It is also necessary that the network must be using the Wi-Fi safe access or WPA2 security protocol. Canon Pixma iX6820 printer to wifi easy step.

Refer to the below steps to set up Canon Pixma iX6820 printer to WI-FI:

  • Turn on the printer
  •  Press and hold the Wi-Fi switch which is located on the forward-facing side of the printer. Hold the Wi-Fi button until the white power light flickers once and then release it.
  •  When the button shows blue light, go to the access point and press the WPS button within 2 minutes

Ways to Setup Canon Pixma iX6820 Wireless Printer on Windows 

  •  Turn on your laptop and Canon Pixma iX6820
  •  Connect your printer to the Wi-Fi by following the above-mentioned steps if you haven’t already
  •  Download the compatible printer drivers from canon’s official website and follow the on screen instructions in order to install them
  •  Once you have the driver installed successfully, go to the control panel
  •  Select ‘Device and Printers’
  •  Click on ‘Add Printer’. You will get a number of options on the screen, select the specific printer’s model that you would like to add
  •  Click on ‘Ok’ to set up the printer

Ways to Setup Canon Pixma iX6820 Wireless Printer on MAC 

  •  Turn on your MacBook, printer, and Wi-Fi
  •  Connect the printer to the same Wi-Fi to which you have connected the MacBook
  •  Download the compatible drivers from the official website of Canon
  •  Install the drivers and while the setup is going on, select ‘Cable less setup option’ and follow the instructions displayed on the screen
  •  Once it has been installed successfully, go to Apple Menu and select ‘System and Preference’
  •  In the next window, you will find the option of ‘Printer and Scanners’. You need to select it
  •  In order to add the printer, click on the plus sign (+)
  •  You will get a list of printers, select Canon Pixma iX6820 and click to add it.

Ways to Setup Canon Pixma iX6820 Wireless Printer with the phone 

  •  Open the ‘LAN Settings’ in your phone
  •  From the ‘LAN Settings’, navigate to ‘Wireless LAN Setup’ and then click on ‘Cable less setup’ and select ‘Ok’
  •  Install ‘Canon ink print jet/ Selphy app’ from the app store or the play store
  •  Once the app has been installed, open it and select ‘+’ icon
  •  Click on the option ‘Register Printer’ which you can find at the bottom of the screen
  •  Click on ‘Printer setup’
  •  Select ‘Auto Connect’ and then click on ‘Yes’
  •  You will be asked to enter the router’s password. Fill it in and click on ‘Ok’
  •  You will see ‘Sent the settings message’ click on OK
  •  Click on your printer’s name which has been detected and enter the name of the canon printer before saving the changes

This is the easiest way to Setup Canon Pixma iX6820 Wireless Printer on different devices without any technical knowledge. However, if you still require any assistance then feel free to get in touch with us.

]]> Have you bought a new Canon Pixma ix6820 printer for home use? Well, the printer is the perfect choice for home as well as office use. It is the versatile printer, it can give you amazing prints, can scan files, support fax features, as well as users, can connect it with their smartphone. However, to use the printer, you will to first unbox it and complete the setup process. Start by selecting a clean and flat surface where you will keep it. You can choose the table near to your PC, and take it out from the box and put it there.

Next, you have to remove all the packaging material and tapes from inside of the printer as well. Now plug the power cord of the printer into a working electric socket. Switch on the printer from the power cord, and press the power button of the printer. Now you need to open the ink cartridges door and align the ink cartridges accordingly. You can also take the printer manual help. When you are done with it, place printing sheets in the input tray and place one page under the scan glass. Now the printer will align and activate its features, now you have to proceed further.

The printer only works when it is connected with the computer or device and with the internet source. Hence, you need to complete the printer wireless connection setup as well, but before this, you need to download the required drivers on your PC. So let us start by setting up printer drivers.

Download and Install Canon Pixma ix6820 Printer Drivers

Printer drivers are very essential to have because they help in sending the command to the printer directly from the system. And after getting the command printer starts printing. If you don’t have the printer you won’t be able to print. Therefore, follow the below steps to complete the setup:

  1. Go to the official website of the Canon printer setup.
  2. Enter your printer Model number and name and locate the right printer drivers.
  3. Next, you need to click ‘Download’ to get the setup file.
  4. When it is completely downloaded then locate the setup file on your computer.
  5. Open the downloads folder and you will find the setup file there.
  6. Now double-click the file and allow it to ‘Run’.
  7. Click on the Install option and keep following the on-screen instructions.
  8. At the end, when the installation process is done, you need to click Finish.

Now restart your computer and check the printer drivers, after this, you will need to connect your printer with the wireless network

Setup Canon Ix6820 Printer With Router Wirelessly

Make your Wi-Fi router is on and having high-speed internet.

  1. Tap on the printer screen, your printer installation process will begin.
  2. Now select the network tab and choose the Wireless Connection tab from the next screen.
  3. Click on the Wireless option from the menu.
  4. Next, choose the installer button and select the Network tab.
  5. Choose the Wireless Setup option and hold the wireless button on the printer and confirm.
  6. Do not release the button until the power lamp flashes twice.
  7. The install will display the list of software, select the software and click OK.

Wait until the wireless printer setup completes, after that you can move ahead to enjoy the amazing features of the printer and get the HD prints. Once the connection with the router completes, your printer screen will show the notification. Now connect the printer with the computer and for this make sure that both the devices are connected with the same network.

Go to the Printers and Scanners window in your computer and click on- Add a printer. From the list select your printer and your computer will establish the connection.

You can also connect the printer wirelessly with your mobile phone, and you can print your favorite pictures directly from your phone.

Connect the Pixma ix6820 Canon Printer With Mobile Phone

  1. To connect your Canon ix6820 printer make sure that the printer is turned on.
  2. The printer will have a WI-FI button once you click that it will enable the WiFi connection.
  3. Noe makes sure that your mobile phone is also connected with the same wifi.
  4. Now go to phone settings add your Canon Pixma printer under scanner and printer option.
  5. Click on Connect and wait till your mobile phone is establishing the connection.

Now your printer is connected with your mobile device as well, now you can easily get the amazing printouts directly from the printer. You can print your screenshots, saves webpages as well as emails. If you have made any presentation or picture collage in your phone, you can print that as well just like a pro and you will get amazing HD prints.

Enjoy printing with the Canon Pixma ix6820 printer!
