Procedure 1: Installing a USB Printer For Windows And iOS

Go through the given instructions to install an USB printer for both Windows and iOS system:

  4. In case, you don’t have the disk, download the drivers from the manufacturer’s website

Procedure 2: Install A Network Printer For Windows

You can connect your printer to a network via wired or wireless ways.

Wired: With a wired Ethernet network cable you can simply connect your device and generally doesn’t require any further network configuration.

Wireless: Connect your printer with a Wi-Fi network to wirelessly connect the device.

Desired Steps You Need To Follow

  1. Open the Control Panel on your Windows
  2. Select ‘Devices and Printers’
  3. Click on Add a Printer
  4. Now, choose ‘add a network, wireless or Bluetooth printer’
  5. Select your wireless printer from the given-list and click Next
  6. Now install the drivers: During installation make sure your system is connected to the internet. In case, you do not have internet, you can use the CD which came along with the printer

Procedure 3: Install A Network Printer For iOS

Follow the steps noted below:

  1. Click on the Apple menu and select System Preferences
  2. Open for Print and Fax option
  3. Tap on ‘+’ symbol to add your printer
  4. Select your network printer from the default tab
  5. Click Add and now your network printer is installed on iOS and you can frequently use your printer for printing from iPhone, iPad or iPod

Procedure 4: Install A Canon Wireless Printer

  1. First, make sure your printer is plugged-in and it is ON
  2. Check if the printer has installation software
  3. Now, connect the printer to the wireless network
  4. Make sure your computer and printer is on the same network

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How Do I Install My Canon Printer To My Computer?

Below are some easy directions for installing your Canon Printer on your personal computer:

  1. Connect your Canon Printer to your PC using USB.
  2. Click on the Start button on your computer system
  3. Click on ‘Devices’ and then, choose the ‘Printers’ option.
  4. Next, you need to select ‘Add Printer’ and click ‘Add a Local Printer’.
  5. Select ‘Next’ and then download printer drivers from the manufacturer’s website.
  6. Click Finish after selecting your printer’s name.

By following these steps, you will be able to complete the Canon Printer installation with ease.

How Do I Setup My Canon Printer Wirelessly?

Have a glance at the instructions that you need to follow carefully for a complete setup of Canon Printer wirelessly!

  1. On the Start menu, you should choose ‘Devices and Printers’
  2. Tap on ‘Add a printer’ option.
  3. Select ‘Add A Network, Wireless Or Bluetooth Printer’ from the ‘Add Printer wizard.
  4. Select your printer and then click ‘Next’.
  5. Install the printer drivers and complete the on-screen instructions and click ‘Finish’ option.

With these steps, you can easily setup Canon printer wirelessly without any kind of obstacles.

How Do I Reinstall My Canon Printer?

  1. Take USB cable to connect the computer and Canon printer.
  2. Turn of the printer.
  3. Press the ‘Start’ button from your keyboard and select the ‘Control Panel’ option
  4. Go to ‘Hardware and Sound’ and then ‘Devices and Printers’.
  5. After that, select ‘Add a Printer’
  6. Choose an option says ‘Local Printer’
  7. Click the “Canon” printer and then ‘Next’.
  8. Next, you should give a name to your printer and click ‘Next’
  9. Click the ‘Finish’ option.

By following these steps, users will be able to reinstall the Canon printer. To ensure its working, you can print a test page.

How Do I Install My Pixma Printer Without The CD?

Walking through the below steps carefully will help you to install Pixma printer without using installation CD:

  1. Firstly, establish a connection between your Pixma Printer and computer using USB Cable
  2. On your computer, click on ‘Start’ option and select ‘Devices and Printers’ option
  3. Choose ‘Properties’ by right-clicking on your printer.
  4. Press ‘OK’ and then follow the on-screen instructions to install the drivers of your Pixma printer.

Once done with the installation of your Pixma printer, you should print a test page to ensure it’s working properly.

How Do I Get My Printer To Connect Wirelessly?

People can easily connect their printer wirelessly. For that, you need to walkthrough the following steps. Check it out:

  1. On your first step, turn on your printer.
  2. Next, hold the Wi-Fi button for a couple of seconds until an alarm light blinks.
  3. After that, press the WPS button if alarm lamp starts blinking blue.
  4. Once the printer established a connection, Wi-Fi and power light stops flashing.

You can have a printout to confirm to make sure your printer has a stable wireless network.

How Do I Install A Printer Driver?

You can’t work on any printer until you install all the necessary drivers. However, you can easily install the printer drivers by following the below-mentioned steps.

  1. For that, you should go to go to Control Panel from your computer
  2. Select ‘Hardware & Sound’ option and choose ‘Device Manager’
  3. Look for your printer and right-on it.
  4. Now, select ‘Run as administrator’ to install the drivers of your printer.
  5. Afterward, click on the properties and then go to ‘Advanced’ option
  6. Reach out to ‘New Driver Tab’ and follow the on screen instructions
