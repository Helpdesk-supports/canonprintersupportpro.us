Where Is The Reset Button On A Canon Printer?

There is a reset button available on the canon printer but it doesn’t mean that you can’t reset your Canon printer.

  1. To do so, you need to turn off your printer first
  2. A ‘STOP’ button appears you should Press and hold it for a couple of seconds.
  3. Then, you will have to press the ‘ON’ button
  4. And, release the ‘STOP’ button.
  5. Again press the ‘STOP’ button five times continuously while holding the ON button.
  6. Lastly, you will have to release all the buttons

You will be able to successfully reset your Canon printer.
