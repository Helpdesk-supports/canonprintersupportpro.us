Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Why Is My Printer Showing Error?

The most common cause when the printer shows printing error is because of the connection issue. Maybe your connection between the printer and the computer is lost.

Check your USB cable and other wire, and if you are sure that all the wires are OK, then try to reconnect your printer. Sometimes, a printer in error state also shows because of the corrupt printer drivers. In this case, you need to download and install your respective printer drivers again.

Where Is The Reset Button On A Canon Printer?

In the canon printer, there is no specific Rest button is given, but you will find the Stop button. In the steps for resetting the printer generally Stop button is used. Some people may often consider the Stop button on the canon printer as the Rest button.

You can follow the list instructional fruitful steps to reset your Canon printer using Stop and Power button.

Why Is The Orange Light Flashing On My Canon Printer?

When a printer is going through the printing error, the Alarm LED light flashes Orange, you can consider this orange light as a warning message. Until the error is not rectified, your printer will continue flashing the light.

If you have noticed a certain pattern in the flashing light, you might get confuse. But the reason behind the number of flashes indicates a certain type of error. And after analyzing the count, it is important to take appropriate steps to solve the issue.

For example, Five Flashes means Printer Head is not installed or Printer Head is defective.

How Do I Clear Error Messages On My Canon Printer?

When your printer shows a message as – Canon printer is in error state, it means there is some issue either within the printer or in the printer software.

Sometimes because of the connection issue, lose wire or USB cable connection, low-speed internet and when printer drivers are not updated or corrupted, your canon printer can show an error message. So to clear the error message and issue you have to follow certain steps.

First, make sure that there is no connectivity issue. Then check the internet router speed.

If something else is the issue causing printer error state error, then you are advised to reinstall the printer drivers. Users can also rectify this error by resetting the printer.

How Do I Fix My Canon Printer From Blinking?

If you find your printer keeps blinking and not performing its printing job, then it means there is some error with your printer. When the printer flashes orange light, means it is indicating some error.

When the printer is in error state it blinks in a certain pattern, so if you notice such pattern then you can perform such tasks to fix canon printer from blinking continuously.
