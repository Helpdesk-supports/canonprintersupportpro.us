  • First of all, turn your printer off and then wait for a while. After than that turn it back ON
  • In case “the printer is not responding Mac” error persists despite restarting the compatible printer, try the below the setting on Mac:
  1. Click on the “Apple” drop-down menu and opt for “System Preferences” from the list
  2. From the “Hardware” section, choose “Print and Fax” and then press on it
  3. Now, click on the “Plus” sign and highlight the printer in the Bonjour
  4. After that, click on the “Add” option

  3. From the Hardware section, choose Print and Fax

  5. When the Mac identifies Canon printer, the latter displays in the list of printers. If your computer is unable to detect the printer, then reconnect the cable to other USB ports on the computer.
