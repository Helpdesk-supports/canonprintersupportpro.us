Effective Steps To Connect iPad Air To Canon Printer

Before trying to Connect iPad Air To Canon Printer ensure that both the devices are on the same wi-fi network. After that, you can start establishing connection in between iPad and Canon printer. To do this, go through the below provided instructions:

  • First and forecast, you have to turn your Canon printer ON by pressing the Power button. Skip this step if the printer is already on
  • Now, disconnect the printer’s Bluetooth or any cable connections as during connection via AirPrint, your printer cannot be connected to your computer via Bluetooth nor can it be connected to a router via Ethernet cable
  • After that, connect your printer to Wi-Fi if your printer is not already connected to a wi-fi network. To choose a strong wi-fi connection, use the printer’s menu
  • Now, open your iPad’s settings by clicking on the “Settings” app icon
  • Now, tap “Wi-Fi”, it is near the top-left side of the screen. Doing this will open your iPad’s Wi-Fi menu
  • Make sure you are connected to the same network as your printer. However, your iPad needs to be connected onto the exactly same wireless network on which your printer connected
  • Now, place your printer just near to the iPad and then print the documents

 Ways To Print With AirPrint

  • Open the app in which the item you want to print is stored. Click on the app’s icon to open it
  • Now, select the page or document you want to print and then click on “Share” icon which is generally present at one corner of the screen
  • Click “Print” option which is shown in a printer-shaped icon at the bottom row of the pop-up menu. Doing so opens the Print menu
  • Tap “Printer”. This will open a list of the compatible AirPrint printers within range
  • Choose your printer that is Canon printer with which you want to print your item
  • At the end, tap “Print” from the top-right corner of the screen. Your selected item will now begin printing
  • You will get a printout in just a couple of second so wait until then

What Is The WPS Button?

WPS stands for Wi-Fi Protected Setup which brings two options including the WPS button and WPS Pin. When it comes to the WPS button, you will have to push it for the first time. Once you press it, it will turn on the discovery process. It will find out the printer device and connects it automatically, in a couple of seconds. Apart from that, you can also make use of the WPS Pin for establishing the connection wirelessly.

Hereafter, you will see a list of available printers and then select your network printer. Follow the on-screen instructions to establish the connection to connect canon printer to WI-FI in simply.

First of all, you should go to the control panel of your Printer. After that, you will have to press the Wireless button. You will get an option says ‘PIN’ option and you need to tap on this PIN. Once you tap on it, you will get the WPS PIN appearing on the display of your computer.
