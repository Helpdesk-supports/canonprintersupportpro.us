Procedure to Hard Reset Canon Printer Error

Follow the below-noted points to fix hard reset canon printer error. Step by step perform all those given points. You will surely be able to terminate this error code from your system. Therefore, have a look at here:

  • First of all power-off your Canon printer device
  • Now, hold “Stop” button for few seconds
  • After that hold “ON” button for two seconds while holding “Stop” button
  • Now, release “Stop” button and press “Stop” button 5 times consistently
  • Release both the “ON” and “Stop” button at same time and wait the printer shutdown
