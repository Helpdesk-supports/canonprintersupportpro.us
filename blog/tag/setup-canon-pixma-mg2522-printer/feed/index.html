]]> The Canon Pixma MG2522 is an all-in-one inkjet printer including scan and copy functions. It works with Windows and macOS.  There is no display screen on the printer and also no networking ability, just only direct USB connection. It is an affordable printer adjustable for home and office premises. Setting up the Canon printer is the initial stage consists of unboxing the printer, downloading the driver, installing the ink cartridges, loading the stack of paper into the input tray to prepare the printer for print job. Once you completely Setup Canon Pixma MG2522 Printer, you can print, fax or scan the document without any hurdles. If you are finding any kind of difficulties in setting up your Canon Pixma printer at your home or office, you can read this blog and follow the instructions as guided here.

Effective Steps To Setup Canon Pixma MG2522 Printer

Go through the given noted points one-by-one if you are looking for the setup procedure of your Canon Pixma MG2522 Printer. Give a glance at once:

  • If you really purchase the Canon Pixma MG2522 printer from the manufacturer, first you should check if it is sealed properly
  • Now, once you confirm the box is sealed properly, the next step would be unbox the printer & check if the entire necessary components are there inside the box
  • Now, remove the packing cover from the printer
  • Take out the printer carefully from the box and place it on a flat surface
  • Peel off the protective tapes from the printer
  • Connect the power cord to the desired port or the wall outlet for Canon PIXMA MG2522 setup printer & power supply
  • After that, power-on the printer by pressing its power button forcefully
  • Unpack the ink cartridges enclosed with the printer package
  • Open the front door of the printer and insert the cartridges into the respective slots
  • Load limited stack of papers into the input tray

Now, you are required to download driver and install it on your operating device. Here is how to download Canon PIXMA MG2522 Driver.

Canon PIXMA MG2522 Driver

  • Download Canon PIXMA MG2522 Driver On Windows
  • Get the right driver for your Canon Pixma MG2522 setup printer from the CD that you received with the purchase of Canon printer or by clicking on Driver Download button on the official site
  • When you visit this site, you will get the latest version of the full-featured printer software that suits the Windows you are operating on your computer or laptop
  • Click on the Driver Download button & let the downloading process start
  • Once you download the driver completely, save it in the destination folder on your computer or laptop
  • The format of the driver file for the Windows OS is .exe file
  • You can install the Canon Pixma MG2522 printer software after getting its downloaded
  • Download Canon PIXMA MG2522 Driver On Mac
  • Avail the printer driver either from the installation CD comes with the printer package or by clicking on Driver Download button from its official site
  • For downloading the driver from the installation CD, make sure that your Mac device has a disc drive
  • The recently manufactured Mac computer or laptop does not contain a disc drive
  • So, use a flash drive to copy & paste the content of the CD into the destination folder on your computer
  • Once you paste the content, a set of files will display on the screen

Double-click on the driver file that you have downloaded earlier and install the Canon Pixma MG2522 printer software on your Mac computer or laptop with following the on-screen prompted instructions. Finally select “Finish” button once the installation process gets done.

Canon PIXMA MG2522 Completely Solutions

  • Connect Canon PIXMA MG2522 Printer Wirelessly
  • Power on your printer by pressing its “Power” button
  • On the operation panel of the Canon printer, click onto the “Setup” option
  • Select Wireless LAN Setup and then press “OK”
  • Select the “Easy Setup” option from the list
  • Choose the name of your router and provide its WEP key
  • Continue with the process by clicking “OK”
  • Install the printer software on your computer or laptop
  • Select “Use the Printer on Network” option while installing the printer driver
  • Follow the instructions appear on the screen and complete the wireless setup on your Canon Pixma MG2522 printer
  • Connect Canon PIXMA MG2522 Printer To Wi-Fi
  • First, turn your printer ON by pressing Power button forcefully
  • Tap and hold the “Wi-Fi” button and release it once the Wi-Fi lamp starts blinking
  • Press the “Black” button
  • Again press the Wi-Fi button until the network lamp flashes
  • Now press the “Color” or “Black” button to complete the Wi-Fi setup

Now, you are successfully done with the Canon Printer Setup process. Both hardware and software setup procedures are mentioned above. In software setup process, you need to connect the printer wirelessly or through Wi-Fi, and download printer driver. After completely perform the setup process, you will able to print the document.
